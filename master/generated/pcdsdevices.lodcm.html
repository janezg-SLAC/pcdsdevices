pcdsdevices.lodcm

LODCM: Large offset dual-crystal monochrometer.

The scope of this module is to identify where the beam is intended to go after passing through H1N, to manipulate the diagnostics, and to remove H1N for the downstream hutches.

It will not be possible to align the device with the class, and there will be no readback into the device’s alignment. This is intended for a future update.
