pcdsdevices.mirror

Offset Mirror Classes.

This module contains all the classes relating to the offset mirrors used in the FEE and XRT. Each offset mirror contains a stepper motor and piezo motor to control the pitch, and two pairs of motors to control the horizontal and vertical gantries.
